Dik-Dik

Once they find that special someone, Dik-diks mate for life. They kick their offspring out of the territory when they reach adult size.

Dik-dik is the name for any of four species of small antelope in the genus Madoqua that live in the bushlands of eastern and southern Africa. Dik-diks are named for the alarm calls of the females.
